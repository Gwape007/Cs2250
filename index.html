Eighty-six 86 by ASATO ASATO

The world of Eighty-six is something that I have been following for the past two years and over the course of 11 current books I do not regret the journey going through and reading it. The story
itself is a great scifi series that plays off themes of equality and discriminations in a way that really makes you think about how some people with certain values really act while giving a nice mix of
action and subtle more hard themes like racism or having to find a reason to live. I still am eager to pick up each new volume of the series when it comes out and seeing where the story takes the characters.

Goblin Slayer by Kumo Kagyu

This yet another series I have been reading for a long time and for its credit started off in a really cruel and off putting way however sticking with the story itself was something I did not regret. The story is a purely fantasy setting with the main character carry trama leaving him with only want to end goblins and showing no interest in anything other. The series for its part moves into something I didn't expect which was growth in the character known as Goblin Slayer and the people he meets and starts to get along with on the way. Something intersting about this series is that no character is given names and rather called by their roles, a couple of examples are: Goblin Slayer, Priestest, Dwarf, Guild girl, Lizard Priest.

Sisyphus II by Ibrahim

Sisyphus II is a song that has a lot of reference to different mythologies which is why I really enjoy it. Paired with the beat and the smooth transitions and the the song being a play on the Sisyphus who for eternatiy having to push a bolder for it to rest then going to to saying the person in the song is the having to replay their time alive and each setnence is something they have done in order to try and break the eternal loop they are in captivated me and is why its one of my favorite songs.

Laydown by Priestest

This is a banger of a song which is something that I keep in my rock and roll playlist. I found this song on a whim and have enjoyed it eversince. The rythm of the song really gives an energetic feel and I feel is a great song just to jam out to while doing anything to keep focus.
